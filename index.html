Experienced General Practice Firm

James J. Foster, P.A. is located in Destin, Florida. Our office represents clients throughout the Florida panhandle and southern Alabama and every level of the Florida and Alabama state court systems.

Our Firm's Approach

Founded in 2001, we opened our practice to provide confident, knowledgeable representation and personal attention to our clients. Unlike larger firms, you will meet directly with an attorney.

Technologically advanced, we have a virtually paperless office. Using technology to our advantage, we are able to keep our clients constantly informed about the status of their cases through the use of secured extranets.

Representing Clients in Negotiation and the Courtroom

With the prevalence of unethical business practices in today's society, it can be difficult to know who to trust. Our firm is dedicated to fighting back for those who have been hurt by others mistakes and wrongdoing, we will aggressively represent your interests. We counsel those involved in matters including :

  • Foreclosure defense
  • Easement and property line disputes
  • Condominium and Homeowners Association law
  • Contract disputes, such as mechanic's liens or failure to pay Association dues
  • Family Law Matters including: divorce, custody, child support, domestic violence, etc
  • Construction Law
  • Trade Regulation
  • Business Formation and Incorportation
  • Insurance Law, including hurricane claims
  • Personal Injury
  • Business Tort Litigation
  • Products Liability
  • DUIs
